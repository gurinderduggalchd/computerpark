GRAPHIC DESIGNING IN COREL DRAW

The Graphic Designing with CorelDRAW course at Computer Park is a practical, industry-focused program designed to help learners master one of the most popular vector graphic design tools used in advertising, branding, and print media. This course provides a solid foundation in the CorelDRAW interface, vector drawing, shapes, typography, and layout design while gradually introducing advanced creative techniques used by professional designers.

Students learn to create a wide range of designs, including logos, business cards, brochures, flyers, posters, banners, product packaging, and social media creatives. Special attention is given to modern design trends such as minimalist branding, flat design, 3D illustrations, clean typography, color psychology, and visual hierarchyâ€”skills that are in high demand across digital marketing and creative industries.

The course also covers essential tools like power clip, blend, envelope, mesh fill, vector tracing, and advanced path editing. Learners gain a strong understanding of pre-press and print production standards, including working with CMYK/RGB color modes, bleed and crop marks, resolution settings, and exporting print-ready formats.

Trending topics like Instagram ads, YouTube thumbnails, corporate branding, and AI-enhanced design workflows ensure students stay aligned with current industry requirements. Real-time assignments and projects help build a professional portfolio, giving learners the confidence and capability to work as graphic designers, freelancers, or brand creators.

By the end of this course, students will be able to design attractive, high-quality graphics for both digital and print media, making it ideal for beginners, entrepreneurs, and professionals looking to sharpen their creative skills.
