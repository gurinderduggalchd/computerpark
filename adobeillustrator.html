GRAPHIC DESIGNING IN ADOBE ILLUSTRATOR

At Computer Park, our Graphic Designing with Adobe Illustrator course is designed to equip students with the creative and technical skills required to excel in today’s modern design industry. Adobe Illustrator is the industry-standard software for vector graphics, logo designing, illustrations, icons, packaging layouts, and digital artworks. This course provides a complete, hands-on learning experience that takes you from the fundamentals of vector drawing to advanced, professional-level design techniques.

Throughout the program, students learn how to work with artboards, shapes, paths, typography, gradients, layers, brushes, mesh tools, and advanced vector effects. You will explore modern design trends such as minimalistic branding, geometric illustrations, flat design, infographic creation, and social media creatives. The course also covers the use of the Pen Tool, Image Trace, 3D tools, blend effects, and pattern creation—empowering you to produce clean, scalable, and print-ready graphics for branding and advertising.

Learners will practice real-world projects including logo design, business card layouts, social media posts, product packaging, poster design, character illustrations, and marketing assets. Special focus is placed on colour psychology, design principles, typography rules, and building a strong visual identity.

By the end of this course, students will have a professional portfolio showcasing their creative work and will be prepared for careers such as Graphic Designer, Illustrator, Branding Artist, and Creative Visualizer. Whether you are a beginner or someone looking to enhance your design skills, this course offers a perfect blend of creativity, tools, and practical experience to succeed in the design world.

Join Computer Park and bring your creative ideas to life with Adobe Illustrator!

Course Outline for GRAPHIC DESIGNING IN ADOBE ILLUSTRATOR

Course Duration: 8 weeks (2 months) 

Course Level: Beginner to Intermediate

Month 1: Foundations of Adobe Illustrator & Core Design Skills

Week 1: Introduction to Illustrator & Vector Basics

       ---  Understanding the Illustrator interface and workspace

       ---  Artboards, navigation, and essential settings

       ---  Vector vs. raster graphics – advantages in design

       ---  Basic shapes, lines, strokes, and fills

       ---  Working with selection tools and transformation tools

Week 2: Mastering Tools & Drawing Techniques

       ---  The Pen Tool in detail – anchor points, handles, curves

       ---  Pencil, Brush, Blob Brush, and Smooth tools

       ---  Shape Builder Tool and Pathfinder operations

       ---  Creating custom shapes, icons, and simple illustrations

       ---  Hands-on mini-project: Icon Set Design

Week 3: Typography & Colour Techniques

       ---  Introduction to typography: fonts, kerning, leading, tracking

       ---  Using the Character and Paragraph panels

       ---  Colour theory, gradients, swatches, and global colours

       ---  Using Colour Guide, Recolor Artwork, and trending colour palettes

       ---  Hands-on mini-project: Typography Poster

Week 4: Working with Layers & Advanced Effects

       ---  Layers, groups, and object management

       ---  Transparency, blending modes & masking techniques

       ---  Gradient Mesh, Pattern creation, and Brushes

       ---  Introduction to 3D tools for modern illustration

       ---  Mini-project: Flat Illustration or Minimalist Artwork

Month 2: Advanced Vector Art, Branding, and Professional Projects

Week 1: Branding & Logo Design

       ---  Understanding branding concepts and visual identity

       ---  Logo design principles and do’s & don’ts

       ---  Creating geometric, modern, and minimal logos

       ---  Using Grids, Golden Ratio, and Symmetry in logo creation

       ---  Project: Complete Logo Design with Variations

Week 2: Illustration Techniques & Creative Artwork

       ---  Character illustration basics

       ---  Isometric illustrations using grids

       ---  Infographic design & data visualization

       ---  Advanced blend tool methods and perspective drawing

       ---  Project: Character or Isometric Illustration

Week 3: Print & Digital Media Designing

       ---  Designing business cards, letterheads, and packaging outlines

       ---  Working with CMYK, spot colours, and print-ready settings

       ---  Social media banners, ads, and post creatives

       ---  Exporting for print and web (PDF, PNG, SVG, EPS)

       ---  Project: Packaging Design or Social Media Campaign

Week 4: Portfolio Development & Final Project

       ---  Compiling a professional graphic design portfolio

       ---  Polishing and optimising project files

       ---  Final comprehensive project (branding kit, poster series, or illustration set)

       ---  Interview & freelance guidance for graphic design careers
